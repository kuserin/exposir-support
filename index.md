## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why does Exposir need camera access?**
A: Exposir uses your camera to measure the light in front of you in real time. The camera feed is never recorded or saved.

**Q: How do I lock the exposure?**
A: Tap the lock button on the main screen. The EV value will be frozen so you can take your time composing your shot.

**Q: How does Long Exposure mode work?**
A: Open Long Exposure mode from the main screen. Select your aperture, ISO, and ND filter, and Exposir will calculate the correct shutter speed and start a countdown timer.

**Q: Does Exposir work with film cameras?**
A: Yes. Exposir is designed for any camera that requires manual exposure settings, including film cameras and cameras with manual lenses.

**Q: Is there a widget available?**
A: Yes. You can add Exposir to your Home Screen or Lock Screen via the widget gallery.
