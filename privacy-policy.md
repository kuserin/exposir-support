# Privacy Policy

**Exposir — Light Meter**
Last updated: April 30, 2026

## Overview
Exposir is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Camera Access
Exposir requires access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of measuring light (EV values). The camera feed is processed in real time on your device and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted.

## Data Collection
Exposir does not collect any personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.

## Third-Party Services
Exposir does not use any third-party SDKs or services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy
Exposir does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
